This folder contains the Power BI dashboard created for the Customer Behaviour Analysis project.

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard provides insights into:
- Total customers and total sales
- Purchase amount by product category
- Customer distribution by age group
- Customer distribution by gender

## Key Visuals
- KPI cards: Total Customers, Total Sales
- Bar chart: Purchase Amount by Category
- Bar chart: Customers by Age Group
- Donut chart: Customers by Gender

## Purpose
The dashboard helps stakeholders understand customer purchasing patterns,
identify high-performing product categories, and analyze demographic trends
to support business decision-making.
